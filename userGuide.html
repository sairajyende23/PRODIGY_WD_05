User Guide

Welcome to Weather App! This user guide will help you get started with using our app to check the weather in your city.

Step 1: Enter Your City

Start by entering the name of your city in the search bar at the top left corner of the page.

Step 2: Check the Current Weather

Once you have entered your city, the current weather conditions will be displayed on the screen. This includes the temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.
